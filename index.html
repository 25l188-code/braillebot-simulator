Mixed-Signal Control Processor

Embedded ARM Cortex-M4F microcontroller optimized for high-performance motor control applications. Features built-in hardware math accelerators, precise PWMs, and dual 16-bit analog-to-digital converters to calculate real-time SCARA inverse kinematics and control loop coordinates at sub-millisecond rates.

Pick & Place Operations

Precision Insertion (Embossing)

Direct solenoid force control driven by precision DAC vectors to punch neat Braille dot geometry without tearing paper fibers.

Geometric Drawing
